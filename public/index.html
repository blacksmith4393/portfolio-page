About Me

Originally a mechanical engineer by training, I have always been eager to understand how things work. However, I was continuously frustrated by the physical and financial limitations I faced when trying to that knowledge to build personal projects. But that all changed when a friend introduced me to the idea of learning programming. He showed me all I need to teach myself to program was an internet connection and a passion for learning. I soon discovered that with a little work I could take advantage of the wealth of resources at my fingertips and begin building things of my own.

I began my programming journey with learning to make static pages with HTML and CSS, but quickly realized that I was really drawn to the logical problem-solving of making my pages dynamic and interactive with JavaScript. I have since worked with other front-end technologies by creating dynamic web apps using JQuery, Angular, and React. Currently looking to bring my passion for learning and work ethic to a full-time role in front-end or full stack development.
